About Me

Born and raised in Tahiti, French Polynesia. PhD in Computer Sciences in Montpellier, France (my thesis was about watermarking in H.264 videos). Currently working in Paris. My main tools are Xcode and Textmate.

When I have free time (aka 'bored', about 5 to 6 hours a day), I play with Arduino and Teensy. When I am *really* bored, I play with Instruments, Jenkins and Cucumber.
